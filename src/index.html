Made for Terps, by Terps.

One of the only course selection applications made specifically for UMD students.

Find and filter classes based on difficulty, Gen Ed requirements, engagement, and subject.

Course information contributed by University of Maryland students just like you.

Powerful course searching

It's never been easier to find the perfect course for your interests. With the ability to search and sort by professor, department, difficulty, interest, and more, you'll be sure to find the courses that fit your needs. All course reviews and ratings are provided by University of Maryland students just like you.

Our course searching functions combine the best of all the other websites out there — for the first time, you can search UMD courses by keyword or topic. The frustrating task of finding interesting courses that fulfill your requirements will soon be a thing of the past.

In-depth reviews and descriptions

All the course information you need is now in one convenient place. View professors, Gen Ed requirements, prerequisites, reviews, and more for the courses you're interested in.

Reviews and ratings submitted by you are shown both cumulatively and broken down by professor to give you the full picture. Leave comments and reviews to let others know what you think about the courses you've taken and give back.

Facebook integration

For the first time, you can log in with Facebook to see the courses and professors your friends have reviewed. Check out the courses they've signed up for, past courses you have in common, and what your friends thought of the courses you're interested in.
